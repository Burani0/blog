Habit 1
BE PROACTIVE

Develop our own power to choose our actions and accept responsibility for our own choices

Habit 2
BEGIN WITH THE END IN MIND

Means that we define the kind of life we want to lead. It's creating clear objectives and laying out a vision or blueprint for what matters most to us and who we want to be.

Habit 3
PUT FIRST THINGS FIRST

This is where we plan and accomplish our goals so that our lives reflect the vision we have for ourselves. Learning and regularly practicing.

Habit 4
THINK WIN-WIN

This is the attitude of seeking mutual benefit in our relationships and interactions with others.

Habit 5
SEEK FIRST TO UNDERSTAND THEN TO BE UNDERSTOOD

This is the ability to truly listen to others to understand and honor their perspectives and realities.

Habit 6
SYNERGIZE

This is the process of working with others in a collaboration and creative way to achieve new and better results.

Habit 7
SHARPEN YOUR SAW

This surrounds all the other habits that give us the power to consistently carry out the other habits in our lives. It's a pattern of daily private victories that create an upward spiral of increasing effectiveness that lifts us to new and more rewarding levels of understanding and living.
